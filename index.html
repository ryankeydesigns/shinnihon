Concept

It is said that about 60 years ago in Japan, the current style of charcoal grilling beef began. In the 60 years since then, various restaurants have worked hard, continuing to study improvements in breeding cattle, selecting suitable meat for grilling, aging methods, slicing perfect cuts, creating sauces and seasonings that pair well with beef and so on.

We also have a flagship store in Nihonbashi, Tokyo where we constantly strive to improve on a daily basis to provide delicious grilled beef at a reasonable price. We wanted Malaysians as well as Japanese people living in Kuala Lumpur to be able to enjoy the same style of charcoal grilled beef that we offer in Japan, therefore we have opened this location here in Kuala Lumpur. For our Japanese customers, we are aiming to achieve a level of quality that they will never have to say, “When I return to Japan, I want to eat delicious charcoal grilled beef” because they will have it right here in Kuala Lumpur.

Expertly selected beef, aging methods, preparation, cutting, as well as the flavor of sauce and our accompanying dishes suitably paired with grilled meat – we invite you to enjoy all of these fine details catered to creating the perfect dining experience for you.
